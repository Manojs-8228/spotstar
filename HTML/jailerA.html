SPOTSTAR JAILER MOVIE

                    

                    Jailer is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language action comedy film directed by Nelson Dilipkumar and produced 
                    by Kalanithi Maran under Sun Pictures. The film stars Rajinikanth in the lead role, alongside Vinayakan, 
                    Ramya Krishnan, Tamannaah Bhatia, Vasanth Ravi, Mirnaa Menon, Yogi Babu and Sunil in supporting roles. 
                    Mohanlal, Shiva Rajkumar and Jackie Shroff play guest appearances. It revolves around "Tiger" Muthuvel 
                    Pandian, a retired jailer, seeking to apprehend an eccentric idol smuggler Varman.
