SPOTSTAR 24 MOVIE

                    

                    24 is a 2016 Indian Tamil-language science fiction action film[5] written and directed by Vikram Kumar.
                    It is produced by Suriya under 2D Entertainment. The film stars Suriya in triple roles, alongside Samantha
                    Ruth Prabhu and Nithya Menen. Saranya Ponvannan, Ajay, Girish Karnad, Mohan Raman, Sudha, Sathyan and Charle
                    appear in the supporting roles. The film follows Sethuraman, a scientist, who invents a time travel watch but 
                    Athreya, his twin brother, does everything he can to get hands on that.
