SPOTSTAR THUNIVU MOVIE

                    

                    Thunivu (transl. Fortitude, also marketed as Thunivu – No Guts No Glory) is a 2023 Indian
                    Tamil-language action heist film written and directed by H. Vinoth, and produced by Boney 
                    Kapoor under Bayview Projects LLP and Zee Studios. The film stars Ajith Kumar, alongside 
                    Manju Warrier, Samuthirakani, John Kokken, Ajay and Veera. It follows Dark Devil and his 
                    team who forms a plan and commits bank heist to find the corporate looted people's money.
