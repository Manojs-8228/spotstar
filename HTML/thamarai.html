SPOTSTAR THAMARAI SONGS

                
                   
                    Tamarai is a Tamil woman poet and film lyricist.Born in Coimbatore , Tamarai is a mechanical engineering graduate.
                    His father was a poet and dramatist. Thamarai, who has given a collection of poems called "Eru Doorum Kadu Lallipal"
                    and can also write short stories. He has authored short story collections "Moonstones" and "A few pages from the 
                    middle of my diary". He has received Tirupur Tamil Sangha Award, Sculptor Award and various other prizes for his 
                    literary works.He composed more than a hundred songs , including famous songs like "Vaseekara, Ajjaya Asura, 
                    Tavathilm Patu Varame, Injerungo...". He has traveled to Sri Lanka and Singapore . Gautham Menon has written most 
                    of the songs in the films.
