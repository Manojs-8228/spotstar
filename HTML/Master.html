SPOTSTAR MASTER MOVIE

                    

                    Master is a 2021 Indian Tamil-language action drama film[4] directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj and produced by XB Film Creators. 
                    The film stars Vijay in the titular role, alongside Vijay Sethupathi, Malavika Mohanan, Andrea Jeremiah, Arjun Das, Shanthanu 
                    Bhagyaraj, and Gouri G. Kishan. It is also co-produced by Vijay's manager, Jagadish Palanisamy, jointly with Seven Screen
                    Studio, which also distributed the film. The film follows John Durairaj "J.D.", an alcoholic professor, who takes a three-month
                    teaching job in a juvenile home and clashes with a ruthless gangster named Bhavani, who uses the children as the scapegoat 
                    for his criminal activities.
