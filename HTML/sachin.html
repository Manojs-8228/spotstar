SPOTSTAR SACHIN MOVIE

                    

                        Sachein is a 2005 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film directed by John, and produced by 
                        Kalaipuli S. Thanu. It is a remake of the director's 2002 Telugu film Neetho.[2][3] The film 
                        stars Vijay in the title role along with Genelia and Bipasha Basu, while Vadivelu, Santhanam, 
                        and Raghuvaran play supporting roles. The film marked the Tamil film debut of Bipasha Basu. 
                        The score and soundtrack is composed by Devi Sri Prasad.[4] Jeeva and V. T. Vijayan handled 
                        the cinematography and editing respectively.
