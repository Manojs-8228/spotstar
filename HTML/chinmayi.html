SPOTSTAR CHINMAYI SONGS

                
                   
                    Chinmayi Sripaada is an Indian singer and voice actress who predominantly works in Telugu and Tamil films. 
                    She rose to fame for her critically acclaimed song "Oru Deivam Thantha Poovae" from Kannathil Muthamittal,
                    directed by Mani Ratnam and composed by A. R. Rahman. She was 15 years old when she made her singing debut
                    in Kannathil Muthamittal. She debuted as a voice actress in Sillunu Oru Kaadhal rendering her voice for 
                    Bhumika Chawla, after being referred by one of A. R. Rahman's sound engineers. She debuted as a composer 
                    with a short lullaby titled Laali, which she also sang and released as part of Instagram's music.
