DEMONTEE COLONY

                        

                        Demonte Colony 2 (also marketed as Demonte Colony II: Vengeance of the Unholy) is an upcoming Tamil-language supernatural horror 
                        thriller film directed by R. Ajay Gnanamuthu and produced by Bobby Balachandran under the banner of BTG Universal, together with 
                        Gnanamuthu Pattarai and White Nights Entertainment. The sequel to Demonte Colony (2015), Arulnithi reprises his role as Srinivasan, 
                        alongside Priya Bhavani Shankar, Archana Ravichandran, Tsering Dorjee, Arun Pandian, Redin Kingsley, Sha Ra, Munishkanth, Meenakshi 
                        Govindarajan and Sarjano Khalid.
