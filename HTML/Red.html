SPOTSTAR RED MOVIE

                    

                    Red is a 2002 Indian Tamil-language action film directed by Singampuli (under the name Ram Sathya)
                    and produced by S S Chakravarthy. The film stars Ajith Kumar and Priya Gill in the lead roles, 
                    while Manivannan, Raghuvaran and Salim Ghouse played pivotal roles. The film's background score 
                    and soundtrack are composed by Deva.Red released on 14 January 2002. It received mixed reviews 
                    from critics and had an average box office performance, but later garnered a cult following.
