SPOTSTAR MANKAATHA MOVIE

                    

                    Mankatha is a 2011 Indian Tamil-language action crime thriller film[7] written and directed by Venkat Prabhu, and produced by
                    Dayanidhi Azhagiri under Cloud Nine Movies. The film stars Ajith Kumar, in his 50th credited film, leading an ensemble cast 
                    which includes Arjun Sarja, Trisha Krishnan, Lakshmi Rai, Anjali, Andrea Jeremiah, Ashwin Kakumanu, Vaibhav, Premji Amaren, 
                    Mahat Raghavendra, Jayaprakash, Aravind Akash and Subbu Panchu. Yuvan Shankar Raja composed the score and soundtrack, with 
                    Sakthi Saravanan working as the cinematographer and the duo Praveen K. L. and N. B.
