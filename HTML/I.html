SPOTSTAR I MOVIE

                    

                    I is a 2015 Indian romantic action thriller film directed by S. Shankar, produced and distributed by V. Ravichandran under Aascar Films. 
                    The film stars Vikram, Amy Jackson and Suresh Gopi in lead roles, while Santhanam, Upen Patel, Ojas Rajani (in her film debut) and 
                    Ramkumar Ganesan portray pivotal roles. The film, told in nonlinear narrative, tells the story of a bodybuilder-turned-supermodel 
                    Lingesan alias Lee, who sets out to exact revenge against his enemies after they turn him into a hunchback by injecting him with I virus.
                    The parts of the film were loosely based on The Hunchback of Notre Dame and Beauty and the Beast
