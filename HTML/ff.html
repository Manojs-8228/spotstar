SPOTSTAR FAST AND FURIOUS MOVIE

                    

                    Fast & Furious, also known as The Fast and the Furious, is a media franchise centered on a series of action films that are 
                    largely concerned with street racing, heists, spies, and family. The franchise also includes short films, a television series,
                    toys, video games, live shows, and theme park attractions. The films are distributed by Universal Pictures.
