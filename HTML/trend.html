SPOTSTAR TRENDING SONGS

                
                   
                    A song is a musical composition performed by the human voice. The voice often carries the melody (a series of
                    distinct and fixed pitches) using patterns of sound and silence. Songs have a structure to them, such as the 
                    common ABA form, and are usually made of sections that are repeated or performed with variation later. A song
                    without instruments is said to be a cappella.Written words created specifically for music, or for which music 
                    is specifically created, are called lyrics. If a pre-existing poem is set to composed music in classical music,
                    it is an art song.
