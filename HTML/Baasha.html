SPOTSTAR BAASHA MOVIE

                    

                    Baashha is a 1995 Indian Tamil-language gangster action film written and directed by Suresh Krissna. 
                    The film stars Rajinikanth, Nagma and Raghuvaran, with Janagaraj, Devan, Shashi Kumar, Vijayakumar, 
                    Anandaraj, Charan Raj, Kitty, Sathyapriya, Shenbaga and Yuvarani in supporting roles. It revolves 
                    around an auto-driver who maintains a humble exterior and avoids violence, but has a dark past which
                    he conceals from his family.
