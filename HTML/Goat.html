GOAT

                        

                            The Greatest of All Time (also marketed as GOAT)[a] is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language science fiction action film directed by 
                            Venkat Prabhu and produced by AGS Entertainment. The film stars Vijay in dual roles, alongside Prashanth, Prabhu Deva, Ajmal Ameer, 
                            Mohan, Jayaram, Sneha, Laila, Meenakshi Chaudhary, Vaibhav, Yogi Babu, Premgi Amaren and Yugendran. It is the penultimate film of 
                            Vijay before he enters politics. The film follows former members of a special anti-terrorist squad and how they address problems 
                            stemming from their previous actions.
