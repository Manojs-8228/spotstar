SPOTSTAR BIRIYANI MOVIE

                    

                    Biriyani is a 2013 Indian Tamil-language action comedy thriller film directed by Venkat Prabhu and 
                    produced by Studio Green.[1][2][3][4] It stars Karthi and Hansika Motwani alongside an ensemble cast
                    including Premji, Nassar, Ramki, Sampath Raj, Madhumitha, Jayaprakash, Prem, and Mandy Takhar. Yuvan 
                    Shankar Raja composed the score and soundtrack of Biriyani, which marks his 100th film, while Sakthi 
                    Saravanan and Praveen-Srikanth handled cinematography and editing, respectively.
